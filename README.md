# HiFi-Stereo-TwoWay-Speaker-3DEnclosure
Desktop HiFi stereo teo-way speaker system, 3D printed PETG enclosure with dual driver crossover design.

Instructions

1:Check the .blend file whether the holes on the front panle fit into the size of your speaker.
2:Purchase all the materials(By File Materials).
3:3D Print the 3D modals out by PETG with 3 mm wall thickness and 50% filling.
4. Place the crossover inside the shell.
5: Drill holes that fit your wires to allows wires connect from AMP to crossover.
6.Connect crossover with your speakers.
7.Close your speaker box (Shell and panel) by 6mmM3screws tight.
8:Connect left and right crossover to left and right channel on the AMP respectively.
14:Power the AMP by the recommanded power supply(Relate to the store you buy it from).
15:Connect 3.5mmAUX on the AMP with your sound source and ENJOY!
